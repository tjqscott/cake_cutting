Cake Cutting Algorithm Visualisations

We have a cake which must be shared between three people


Archie splits the cake into three pieces that he wants equally




Bobby picks the two pieces that he likes the most




And trims the best piece so that the pieces are equal




Then Chloe picks her favourite piece from Bobby's two favourites




Bobby gets piece which has been trimmed so Chloe gets to cut the trimming




Chloe cuts the trimming into three pieces which she values equally




Bobby picks his favourite, then Chloe, leaving Archie with the last piece




We add the trimming to the main slice for each player




Leaving every player feeling as though they got their fair share
